Year Group Problems

Reception (Ages 4-5)

Focus: Simple counting, number bonds to 5, adding and taking away with numbers up to 10

Year 1 (Ages 5-6)

Focus: Number bonds to 20, simple addition and subtraction within 20, introduction to multiplication (2, 5, 10) and division

Year 2 (Ages 6-7)

Focus: Addition and subtraction facts up to 20 and derived facts to 100, recall of 2, 5, and 10 times tables and related division facts
